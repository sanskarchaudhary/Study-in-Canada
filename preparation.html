Process to Come Canada

Step 1: Check Eligibility

Check if you are eligible to apply for a Canadian visa or immigration program. Use the Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada (IRCC) online tool to find out.

Step 2: Choose the Right Program

Choose the right immigration program for your needs, such as Express Entry, Provincial Nominee Program, or Family Sponsorship.

Step 3: Gather Required Documents

Gather all required documents, including passport, birth certificate, marriage certificate, education credentials, and language test results.

Step 4: Create an Online Profile

Create an online profile on the IRCC website and submit your application.

Step 5: Pay the Fees

Pay the required fees for your application, including the processing fee and the right of permanent residence fee.

Step 6: Wait for Processing

Wait for your application to be processed. This may take several months or even years.

Step 7: Medical Exam and Police Certificate

Undergo a medical exam and obtain a police certificate from Nepal and any other country where you have lived for more than six months.

Step 8: Interview and Approval

Attend an interview at the Canadian embassy or consulate in Nepal, and wait for your application to be approved.

Step 9: Move to Canada

Once your application is approved, make travel arrangements to move to Canada and start your new life.
